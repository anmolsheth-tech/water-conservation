Water Scarcity Impact

Impact of Water Scarcity

1. Health Issues: Lack of clean water leads to diseases like cholera, dysentery, and typhoid. Poor sanitation increases infant mortality rates.

2. Food Shortages: Agriculture relies on water, and scarcity leads to crop failures. Livestock suffers, leading to reduced food supply and increased prices.

3. Economic Consequences: Industries depending on water (textiles, manufacturing, agriculture) slow down or shut down, leading to job losses and economic instability.

4. Environmental Degradation: Rivers and lakes dry up, affecting aquatic life. Deforestation and desertification increase due to lack of water for vegetation.

5. Social Conflicts: Water scarcity leads to disputes between communities, states, or even nations. Rural populations migrate to cities, increasing urban overcrowding.
